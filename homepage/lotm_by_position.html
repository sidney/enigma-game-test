Levels of the Month -- Archive

Each month, we take a closer look at excellent Enigma levels. Excellent levels are those with the highest average user ratings and the greatest number of ratings altogether. Thus it is your vote that determines the Level of the Month. So please rate the levels you play and do not forget to submit your ratings together with your scores at the end of each month.

These are the featured Levels of the Month. The value in front is the current user rating, the number in brackets is the number of people who rated this level.
