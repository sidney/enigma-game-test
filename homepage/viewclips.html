Movie Clips

Thanks to our active player community we are able to offer you a rapidly increasing number of movie clips. We hope that the clips will give you an insight into the versatile features of Enigma and will show you how other players master levels that look outright unsolvable on first sight.

On this page we offer download links to those clips the Enigma team produced itself. You can download them and review them with any Flash Video supporting player like MPlayer, Realplayer, VLC media player, etc.

Downloading of large movie clips is useful, as you will likely review them many times. But you can view them, as well as numerous additional user clips, directly in this embedded player:
