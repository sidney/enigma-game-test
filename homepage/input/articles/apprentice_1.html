The Apprentice — "You are welcome"

Interested friends who ask me, what's about Enigma, what is it "like", whether the game is "worth the while", all these many levels … I say to them: Be glad, you still have all of them to play! The apprentice looks perplexed at the first twelve "previews" of the opened level pack and starts "Welcome"—and opens "his" first oxyds, still unsure in the handling of the marble that now is under his thumb. Was this all? Yes—Level finished. So easy? Yes—in some respect every level is a "Welcome"-level … with more or less obstacles! Light up the oxyds—and, as a diversion, meditate in "meditation levels": make "the" marble "stay". The preview shows the apprentice, still lucky about the solved "Welcome", that every enclosed level differs in color, composition, shape …, and at the latest during the first contact with the "death's-head" it realises "landscapes" of varying challenge, menaces, that seem limitless in their evolution: Over 1000 levels! And then he sees: Yes—I "know" the level = x% solved, but there is still much to do—fortunately! And thanks to all those that made this possible. And maybe, some day, he starts to "make it possible" as well: You are welcome!

Mecke
