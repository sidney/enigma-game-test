Today's level is "Decorations" by Andreas Lochmann.

Christmas and winter solstice approach, Hanukkah started Thursday, and people decorate their homes and streets in many parts of the world. Lights, be they electric or lights from candles, play the overwhelming role, due to the Northern Hemisphere's dark winter, but also as promise for the advent or return of light; be it physical or symbolical.

The assembly of the cheerful lights however, sometimes tends to shed less cheer on the assembler, who, tripping over cables and blowing fuses, tries to find the one last missing power strip ...
